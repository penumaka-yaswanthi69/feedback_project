Why Feedback?

Feedback improves learner confidence, motivation to learn and ultimately,
learner's attainment. It's also what your people want - 65% of employees say
they want more feedback. Feedback comes in many shapes and forms.
You can deliver feedback episodically, in isolated instances or on an ongoing basis.
